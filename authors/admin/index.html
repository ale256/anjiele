Anjie Le

Anjie Le majored in mathematics at the University of Cambridge. Her work mainly focuses on applying mathematics to the biomedical world. She has done two projects on breast cancer and one on canine CT scans; two on ultrasound imaging, one on CT and one on Whole Slide Images.

As a person, Anjie has a broad spectrum of interests. Quantum computing and Langlands program are something she was keen on. Philosophy, psychology, economics and criminology are what she enjoys reading about in her spare time. Snowboarding, land surfing, swimming, horse-riding and Taekwondo are what she may do when she wants to exercise. She plays the piano and guitar. She enjoys visiting different places. She has three cats and a dog.
